Abdel Fattah El-Sisi❤️

Abdel Fattah Saeed Hussein Khalil El-Sisi was born on November 19 , 1954 in Cairo's Gamaleya neighborhood, to a father named Said Hussein Khalil and a mother named Souad Ibrahim, and his family origins are descended From Menoufia Governorate.He received his primary education at Al-Bakri School, then preparatory and secondary schools, before joining the Egyptian Military College, where he graduated in 1977. He received a master's degree in military science from the Egyptian Command and Staff College in 1987, and another master's degree from the British Command and Staff College in 1992, in addition to a college fellowship He was a Senior War Fellow from Nasser Higher Military Academy in 2003 and a Fellow of the U.S. Senior War College in 2006.
